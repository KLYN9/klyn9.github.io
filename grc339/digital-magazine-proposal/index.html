Introduction

tteok.

tteok. is a digital magazine that speaks to the artistry of Korean rice cakes (떡). This dessert celebrates delicate textures and vibrant colors wrapped in the history of Korea. In this publication, there is an exploration of the traditional and modern evolution of this glutionous rice-based dessert. Together, tteok. curates the visuals and stories that reflect the quiet beauty and craftmanship behind each piece. tteok. is where food becomes art, combining the soft, subtle, and deep parts of what it means to be Korean.
